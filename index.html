What the survey measures

The official survey combines two well-known psychology scales. Empathizing reflects interest in understanding other people’s emotions and thoughts. Systemizing reflects interest in analyzing patterns, rules, and how systems work. Baron-Cohen’s research links these traits to sex differences, autism spectrum conditions, and — in the YourMorals version — political psychology.

On YourMorals.org, researchers ask whether liberals and conservatives differ in these traits, and whether those patterns look different for men and women. Scores run from 1 to 4 on each dimension, with higher numbers meaning stronger preference or ability.

How to use this site

  1. Register and take the Systems & Feelings Survey at yourmorals.org (under “Explore Your Morals” after signing in).
  2. Note your systemizing and empathizing scores from the results page.
  3. Come back here and enter those scores to see a comparison chart with demographic filters.
